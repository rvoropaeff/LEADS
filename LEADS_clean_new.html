LEADS bivariate analysis - Mentorship

Author

Rebecca Voropaeff 

This report observes the potentail impact of mentorship on success factors for
LEADS scholars. The outcome X variable of success factors will be compaired
against the Y variables 'priaccess' or 'My primary mentor of mentoring team is
accessible', and 'prioverall' or 'Please rate the overall effectiveness of your
primary mentor.' The data is unaggregated. 
The LEADS survey has been broken down into subscales that will be assessed in
the following order: 

    Burnout
    Creativity
    Motivation
    Passion and Interests
    Leadership
    Research Skills Inventory
    Professionalism
    Research Experience
    Networking/Socail Capital
    Job Satisfaction
    Career Satisfaction
    Career Adapt-Abilities Inventory
